Art Exhibition

This Global Art Exhibition is a curated online art gallery headquartered in Jaipu,India. Founded in 2006, It is an e-commerce platform that connects artists directly with collectors on its website. The company positions itself as an approachable and convenient alternative to the brick-and-mortar contemporary art gallery


Exhibition starts from 11th October.

An art exhibition is traditionally the space in which art objects (in the most general sense) meet an audience. The exhibit is universally understood to be for some temporary period unless, as is rarely true, it is stated to be a "permanent exhibition". Such expositions may present pictures, drawings, video, sound, installation, performance, interactive art, new media art or sculptures by individual artists, groups of artists or collections of a specific form of art.



 






HISTORY

The company was founded by Shubham Yadav, Dhwani Kshatriya and Supriya Gupta.The three met in a business class at the IT company Infoobjects.pvt.ltd. Sharing an interest in the arts, they developed the concept for online art gallery through the Entrepreneurship Program. The artgallery.com website launched in September 2006. Originally seen as a way to help recent art school graduates find buyers, it began with five artists and 25 pieces of original art. Securing outside capital in 2008, the company shifted its focus to mid-career and emerging artists from around the world

This Exhibition was an early entrant to the business of selling original art online. At the time, many art critics questioned the concept. A 2007 Wall Street Journal article called "the online art marketplace as mottled as a Monet water scene." By 2012, the online art market was valued at $870 million. In 2014, the estimated value of the online art market grew to $2.64 billion, accounting for 4.8% of the estimated $55.2 billion value of the global art marke.
